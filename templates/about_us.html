What is VendorVerse?

VendorVerse is an innovative web platform connecting local vendors in Tralee with JRI America employees.
Our service streamlines the booking process, allowing vendors to sell their goods and services during available times.

Our Mission

We're committed to supporting local businesses, fostering community engagement, and helping small enterprises thrive through our employee network.

Our Company

JRI America, Inc., located at Innovation Works 2, Kerry Technology Park, Tralee, Co. Kerry, V92 C6DD, is a subsidiary of the Japanese IT financial services company, Japan Research Institute Ltd, which is the specialist IT subsidiary of Sumitomo Mitsui Financial Group(SMFG).

How to use our site?

Vendor Registration

Vendors sign up and provide essential details such as business name, contact number, and business description.
Our Admin team reviews applications for quality and relevance, and you will recieve an email once your account has been approved.

Booking Process

Approved Vendors view their available days on the calendar.
Once a suitable day is selected, Vendors fill out a Booking Form, specifying location, requirements, and discount availability.

Connecting with Customers

Approved bookings are visible to all Employees on their calendar.
Vendors gain exposure and have a date to engage with potential customers.
