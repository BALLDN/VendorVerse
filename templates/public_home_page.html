Hello/Konnichiwa, and Welcome to VendorVerse.

What is VendorVerse?

VendorVerse is a web service that allows local Vendors in the Tralee area to book available times to sell their Goods or Services to the employees of JRIA.

JRI America, Inc., located at Innovation Works 2, Kerry Technology Park, Tralee, Co. Kerry, V92 C6DD, is a subsidiary of the Japanese IT financial services company, Japan Research Institute Ltd(JRI), which is the specialist IT subsidiary of Sumitomo Mitsui Financial Group(SMFG).

How to use our site?

Vendors may sign up and offer their availability to the company through booking forms. Vendors must input their information, such as 'Business Name', 'Contact Number' and 'Description of Business', when regstering their account.
Once accepted by our Admin team, they are able to view their available days on the Calendar. Once a suitable day has been selected, they must fill out a Booking Form, which includes information such as 'Location', 'Requirements for Vendor' and 'Discount Availability'.
Once accepted by our Admin team, this booking is available to be viewed by all Employees and the Vendor has a date to gain customers.

Why do we provide this Service?

We provide this service to help local businesses gain customers and work with the local community.
We allow small businesses to gain large amounts of customers through our employees.
